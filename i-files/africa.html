African Journal

Between September 1998 and December 1999 I served as a volunteer at a number of development and social work projects in and around Pretoria, South Africa. The experience has been overwhelming, shocking, enlightening and highly gratifying all at once. While I was "living Africa" I felt the need to share what moved me with all my friends out there -- and this online journal was born. Below you find all the reports I sent back to the "outside world". Enjoy reading, and let me know what you think!
